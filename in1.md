### Introduction to Financial Ratios

![](img/in407.png)

Financial ratios save us a lot of time. A lot of time is required to analyze one company so think of analyzing a hundred companies because at end of the day we are looking for the best-performing companies to invest our money and become part of the company. Hence it is a tedious process so therefore we come up with Financial ratios to at least filter out the companies. Just understand what these ratios mean and represent. Also, have some thumb rules for the thresholds beyond which the company becomes acceptable or unacceptable as an Investor.  

It is the first level of analyzing the company using financial ratios. There are many types of ratios are available but we will look into only the important ones and safely ignore which are not significant.
