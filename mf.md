### What is Risk?

2 types of Risk

* Positive Risk - losing the profits (unrealised profits) due to some incidents and all profits vanish. Unless profit is booked it is unrealised and carry risk of losing those profits
* Negative Risk - Value lower than the acquisition price. Something bought and after that value gone down

ITC is more value stock because of its Cigarette business which brings in huge profit. But because of Govt. regulations and increase in taxes ITC always like cat on wall. So investors feel risky stock and its value is often cheap. Hence considered value stock. Also compare P/E ratio of ITC with its peers. Lesser P/E when compared to industry P/E & peers P/E means then its price is more cheap

* ITC P/E - 19.2
