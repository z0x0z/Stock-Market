Pivot points are based on historic price points and hence does not change  
Indicators are based on current price points and hence not reliable  

**Lagging indicators -** Lagging indicators are tools used by traders to analyse the market using an average of previous price action data. eg. MACD, RSI, SMA, Stochastic

**Leading indicators -** A leading indicator is a technical indicator that uses past price data to forecast future price movements in the market. eg. Fibonacci retracements, Support and resistance levels
